About

Pitch

Do you like prop-hunt? Do you like Dota? This mod is made for you!

Inspiration

The multples prop hunt mods of all the Valve games.

Concept

Basically the prop hunt is a Hide and Seek game where the players who hide have the appearance of objects in the scenery and must therefore innocently place themselves among it. The seeker must find all those who are hiding before the time limit. In addition, when a player is "found", he is placed in a capture area and can be released by his comrades.
