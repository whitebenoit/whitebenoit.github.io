Xplosive Truck

GameJam: "Le salaire de la peur" (french movie)

Learn More

About

Made in 2 and a half days during a Game Jam around the themes of cult movies, Xplosive Truck is a game based on "The Salary of Fear".

Pitch

You drive a truck loaded with nitro glycerin that you have to take to a burning oil well. Every obstacle is a danger, every turn is a risk, every turn can make you explode. But if you arrive fast enough and on time, you can receive a dollar bonus that will allow you to leave the country permanently and fly to Valparaiso!

Inspiration

The film "Le Salaire de la peur" by Henri-Georges Clouzot in which four people agree to drive two trucks loaded with Nitroglycerin in order to have enough money to leave the dead-end place in which they are stuck.

Concept

An "anti-racing" game where the whole gameplay pushes you to drive carefully so that the nitro gauge does not exceed the critical threshold. But in which you are encouraged to go fast to win a bigger bonus. In our game, you will spend time with your eyes alternating between the road and the nitroglycerin gauge to avoid ending up in a fireworks display.
